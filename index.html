How I work
01 — Systems thinking
I map the system before I design the screen

I start with operational context — user roles, pressure points, decision moments, and dependencies. The interface is always the last thing I design, not the first.

02 — Operational UX
I design for real conditions, not ideal flows

Real users work under speed, pressure, distractions, and edge cases. I test for the moments that break a flow and design backward from there — not from a perfect scenario.

03 — Design leadership
I reduce uncertainty for the whole team

I document decisions, surface trade-offs, and align product, engineering, QA, and stakeholders through clear rationale — not just screens. The team should always know what we're building and why.
